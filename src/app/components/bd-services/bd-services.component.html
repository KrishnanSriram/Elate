Schedule & Information

Welcome to the first Columbus Lin-ning badminton tournament at Worthington, Ohio. Ready to take the Linning challenege?

Schedule

Tournament will start of May 4th at 3:00PM. With as many as 15 teams, each team will play 2 games in their pool. Top 1 in each pool qualify to Knock_off's.

Parking

Parking is available in plenty for the tournament. Please arrive well before time and park in the lot, front of the center. If the parking is full, there's more available at the back and in the church across the road.

Photo & Video

We plan to record the tournament, although video recording will be selective, feel free to bring your equipement to record your games or the entire tournament. Pictures of the tournament will be made available in this site at a later point in time.
